Hello! I'm Nikita, currently studying Computer Science at UC Berkeley. I am a Bay Area native, FRIENDS addict, and Emoji enthusiast. My greatest passion is design. Whether it be graphic, web, or product, I have always had an interest in creating aesthetically pleasing, functional designs, platforms, and spaces. Ultimately, I aspire to make an impact in the user experience, user interface, and human-computer interface realms of design and computer science. My hobbies include playing soccer, singing, photography, and videography. I am an avid advocate for female empowerment, and aspire to travel the world as much as possible, as soon as possible. This website serves as an interactive diary of my life - a place where I can record all my experiences and accomplishments, so when I'm older and have time on my hands, I can look back and relive them through all that I posted. I hope you enjoy my small niche on the internet.
